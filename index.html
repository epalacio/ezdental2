Our six steps for Quality Control

Contour

Restorations are contoured mimicking nature to harmoniously blend in with existing dentition. Careful attention given to over/under contouring restorations preventing gingival inflammation and recession.

Contacts

Models are double-poured to check contacts on uncut models.

Occlusion

Restoration thicknesses are checked and maintained for adequate strength. Models are set to your bite registration checking centric, lateral and protrusive excursions..

Dental Anatomy

Dental Anatomy will be matched to dentition style, with natural alignment, occlusal/incisal tables and tooth morphology.

Margin

Margins are closed and thinned under a microscope and used to trim your dies, waxed and finished. Ensuring a precision fit of 40 microns on average. Careful attention is given to proper tooth morphology and especially emergence profile at the margin for excellent tissue compatibility.

Finishing

Glazed and characterised to a natural surface texture and/or highly polished to a mirrored surface free of scratches, pits or sharp angles.

Our Product

Our restorations have natural contours, embrasures, occlusion, with well-defined, precise margins. Restorations fit and function properly, stable in position, and are comfortable for you to wear. Restorations have highly polished surfaces, resistant to plaque formation.
