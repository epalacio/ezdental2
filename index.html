About us

Who we are

In association with John Bell Dental Specialist Prosthodontist and formally known as “Image Dental”. We have been serving the town of Papakura for over 20 years. Our mission is to produce excellent restorations to ensure the wellbeing of the patient and the satisfaction of the dentist.

Our Quality

We deliver the finest possible restorations using the best materials from Germany. We know how important your dental practice is by not having a case deliver on time and not being served by a reliable dental laboratory can destroy a morning or an afternoon of scheduled appointments. We strive to produce quality aesthetics. Our goal is to provide crowns that look exactly like a natural tooth.

Our products

We are a small practice that focuses on quality and long-lasting personal relationships. By communicating directly about the case, we can avoid wasting time with a middleman in a big laboratory environment. By sending us a case or two, you will find out for yourself why we are different to than any other lab in the city.
